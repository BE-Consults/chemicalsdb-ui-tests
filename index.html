Usage Guidelines

Cards & Containers

Use shadow-sl for standard cards. Creates subtle depth without distraction.

Navigation

Fixed headers and sidebars use shadow-sl for separation from content.

Buttons

Primary buttons use shadow-sl to indicate clickability.

Modals & Dialogs

Use shadow-sl-lg or shadow-2xl to overlay content prominently.

Dropdowns & Popovers

Elevated components floating above content need shadow-sl-lg.

Hover States

Interactive elements lift with shadow-sl-hover on hover.
